# Product expiry tracker idea

fridge tracker, cupboard tracker, to detect when things go out of date

**Basic Idea**

Take receipt picture -> Compare to db of expiry dates -> Notify when item/s expire
  
Also can apply to medicine, cosmetics, garden/outdoor products, or any *physical* product that can expire.

**Key Features**

Digitial receipts! (including snapshot of original receipt, and ocr)

Remove identifiers (i.e black out receipt id and/or barcodes)

language preferences, customisable ui (widgets and whatnot)

Product preferences*** (e.g only food products, particular cuisines)

OPTIONAL: Temperature tracking over the next few days, alert users of certain products they have that could go off quickly in higher temps.

**Data requirements**

Country, email, name
